Geneva Lake

Lake Geneva (or Lac Léman) is a crescent-shaped lake shared between France and Switzerland, and overlooked by the Alps. Geneva, the Swiss city at its southern tip, is a diplomatic hub with luxury shops and a cobbled old town.

Location

201 Wrigley Drive, Lake Geneva, WI 53147, Switzerland

Attractions

  • numerous luxurious resorts, stunning beaches, and natural beauty
  • one of the world’s great natural harbours
  • UNESCO World Heritage site
  • hosts 1,600 performances every year including ballet, opera, theatre, dance, music and comedy as well as children’s shows and more
